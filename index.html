Asgard gods & goddess

Skadi

is the formidable Jötunn goddess of winter, mountains, hunting, and skis, known for her fierce independence and love for the wild, cold wilderness.

Thor

the mighty God of Thunder and son of Odin, courageously defends Asgard and Midgard with his legendary hammer, Mjölnir.

Loki

the cunning Norse god of mischief, trickery, and illusion, a complex figure known for both causing chaos and occasionally aiding the gods.

Hela

the formidable Norse Goddess of Death and ruler of the underworld realm of Helheim, known for her immense power and dominion over the deceased.
